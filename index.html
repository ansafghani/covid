CoviDoNATION Advantages

Transparency

No money involved and products will be delivered directly by donatekart, thus complete transparency.

Need Based Donation

Just tell us the products that are in need to your organisation we will make sure that you get them all and you just focus on the work you do.

Free Platform

Completely free for organisations to run any number of campaigns where as crowdfunding platforms charges more than 10% of raised money as fees.

About Us

CoviDoNATION is a unique platform that allows individuals to help the people in need. Through CoviDoNATION, NGOs or individuals display the products they are in immediate need of. Donors can choose the organization that they wish to support and donate the products they want to. The process of buying these products is extremely convenient and is completly safe.
